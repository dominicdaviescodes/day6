Where are the album covers when they're not showing? They're off screen by about 400% which will fool even the largest screen sizes. This is acheived by using a transform/tranistion effect along the X axis. When the boxes come back into view the transition is smoothed by 'transform 0.4s ease' properties.

How will they appear dynamically? We need to bring the album covers back to their home position (0), instead of off screen at 400% and -400% (which coveres us in both left and right directions).

We style a 'show' class, transform: translateX(0). The clever bit here is not adding the show class but knowing when to. In JavaScript we are listening for a scroll event (no clicking today) that will trigger when we scroll and a new 1/3 of the screen is revealed, in comes an image.
